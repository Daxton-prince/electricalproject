About Dax Electricals

DAX Electrical is an electrical contractor serving domestic, commercial & industrial sectors in Kenya ,kiambu county juja constituency ,including local councils, manufacturing, offices, schools and hospitals. We pride ourselves with our professionalism, attention to detail and quality customer service.


DAX Electrical has gained a reputation for quality workmanship and for providing a wide range of electrical services personalised to our clients’ specific requirements. We are able to take your early concept through the design process to achieve the final product. We offer value-for-money services where cost, specification, flexibility and attention to detail are paramount.
